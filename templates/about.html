Our Story

From Passion to Excellence

Printagram Printing Services was founded in 2019. A tiny print shop opened its doors with the simple and clear goal of delivering high-quality printing services. A little more five years later, that humble print shop has grown into one of our town's leading corporate stationery printers.

It all started when Mr. Jose Raphael and Ms. Mica, inspired by their own family history in the printing industry, decided to open a boutique print shop dedicated to offering a bespoke printing experience for clients while also producing a high-quality end product. That concept became a reality in 2019, when Printagram Printing Services was established.

Printagram's manufacturing facility is conveniently located on National Road Panilao Pilar and uses modern technologies. Their creative arrangement of machines for printing allows them to efficiently manage their clients' printing demands, from business cards, flyers, tarpaulins, and signages to full-color marketing materials, while always focusing on quality.

Printagram's unwavering commitment to print quality and service has attracted exceptional customers, including many of the city's largest corporations, restaurants, universities, colleges and agencies. We look forward to meeting with you to discuss how we can effectively fulfill your printing needs.
